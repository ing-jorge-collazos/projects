1. Work with a friend to practice repetition.

John: (Have you seen a red pen anywhere?)
Tina:I’m sorry, I didn’t catch that.
John: (I said, ‘Have you seen a red pen?’)
Tina: Could you say that again?
John: (Red pen, see it anywhere?)
Tina: I’m sorry ... I still didn’t get that.
John: (I said, ‘Have you seen a red pen anywhere?’)
Tina: Could you say that again?
John: (Red pen, see it anywhere?)
Tina: One more time?
John: (Have you seen a red pen anywhere?)
Tina: No, sorry. Nothing.
John: (Have you seen a red pen anywhere?)
Tina: Look! I can’t hear a word you’re saying.
John: I said, ‘Have you seen a red pen?’ Oh, there it is!
