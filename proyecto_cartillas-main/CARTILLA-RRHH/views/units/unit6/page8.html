7 Main Benefits of Labor Welfare
Provision of Labor Welfare activities brings an all-round development of workers. In particular, these measures bring benefits to the workers and to the society
Some of the main benefits of labor welfare are:
Improved Industrial Relations, Increase in the General Efficiency and Income, High Morale, Creation of Permanent Labor Force, Improvement in the Mental and Moral Health, Change in the Outlook of Employers and Social Benefits.
